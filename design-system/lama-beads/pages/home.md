# Home Page Override

## Page Job

Home is the brand and mood page. It should make Châu Ngọc Phúc feel premium, calm, and material-focused before asking users to browse.

## Structure

1. Cinematic scroll video hero.
2. One short brand promise.
3. Small curated collection preview, no more than 4 products.
4. Craft/process or sourcing story.
5. Contact handoff.

## CTA Rules

- One primary CTA in the first viewport: view collection.
- Contact can exist as a secondary text link or subtle button, but not with the same visual weight as the primary CTA.
- Do not show product shortcut cards inside the hero if the next section already shows product cards.

## Media Rules

- Hero media should be video or one strong image, not multiple competing thumbnails.
- Avoid repeating the same product preview image in hero shortcuts and collection cards.
- If a future campaign video exists, Home can feature it; product-specific videos belong on product detail pages.
